About Me

Like I said on the About the Site tab, I am currently a Computer Engineering student at Iowa State University. I enjoy software development for many reasons; being able to solve problems in a logical manner is one of the most important reasons.

Another thing about software development is that it is rewarding. After working for many hours on a problem, the smallest breakthrough feels amazing. Being able to make people's lives easier through the software they use is also a great feeling. For example, creating an app for mobile phones that allows the user to easily see the next time a bus will arrive at a certain bus stop will influence many people.
